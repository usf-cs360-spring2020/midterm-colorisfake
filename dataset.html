Fire Department Calls for Service

This dataset inculdes all fire unit responses to calls in San Francisco, California. Each response includes the call number, incident number, address, unit identifier, call type, disposition, and relevant time intervals. This dataset is updated daily and includes over 5.21 million responses.

Relevant Columns in this Dataset

Column Name Decription
Call Number A unique 9-digit number assigned by the 911 Dispatch Center
Unit ID
Incident Number A unique 8-digit number assigned by the 911 Dispatch Center
Call Type Call Type
Call Date Date the call is received at the 911 Dispatch Center
Watch Date Watch date when the call is received.
Address Address of incident.
City City of incident.
Zipcode of Incident Zipcode of incident.
Station Area Fire Station First Response Area associated with the address of the incident.
Original Priority Initial call priority.
Priority Call priority
Final Priority Final call priority.
Location Location of incident.
